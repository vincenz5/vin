Describing my passion

I've held a passion for sustainability, focusing on sustainable energy, ever since I was 13 years old and began to read the scientific research of humanity's effect on the Earth's environment. It seemed as clear back then as it does now that we are on track for nothing less than the final generations of human civilizations. Many in my generation either feel hopeless about or no need to change this trajectory. Many others of us feel compelled to dedicate ourselves to a solution. I like to believe I'm in the second group.
